About

ROSHI was the first living creature to be launched into space and orbit the earth. This project pays homage to her and the significance of this amazing feat kicking off humankind’s journey to become a multi-planetary species. We’re confident that dogs, like ROSHI, will join us on the journey.

ROSHI the Space Dog coin honors this brave dog

and the efforts all living things have made for space exploration.
